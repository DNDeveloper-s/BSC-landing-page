ADMISSION PROCEDURE


Interested Candidates can obtain Application forms from Admission Office at IMS, Ghaziabad. Candidates can also fill the Online inquiry form by clicking on

http://www.imsuc.ac.in/ apply-online-query.php

Eligibility Criteria


  • The candidate must have passed intermediate /10+2 with biology/math with at least 45% marks for general and OBC and passing marks for SC & ST candidates.

  • Candidates appearing for 10+2 examination or awaiting their results are also eligible to apply. Their candidature however, shall be considered subject to the clearance of the qualifying examination.

Selection Procedure:


  • The admission to the B.Sc. (H) Biotechnology and B.Sc. (H) Microbiology program will be done on the merit (based on the aggregate marks at 10+2 level) declared by the CCS University.

How to Apply


  • Apply online form the website www.imsuc.ac.in.

  • Applications will be shortlisted by IMS, Ghaziabad on the basis of graduation merit.

  • Shortlisted candidates will be asked to appear for GD/PI. Admissions as per University Guidelines.

Procedure


  • Registration Process starts from January 2019

  • Candidates seeking admission in B.Sc. (H) Biotechnology or B.Sc. (H) Microbiology are required to meet the Admission Counselor in the Institute Premises.

  • Admission is confirmed on the basis of merit & interaction.

  • Candidate having 1 or 2 Years gap after XII need to submit a Gap Year Certificate.

Documents Required


  • Photocopy of X & XII mark sheets (With Originals)

  • Photocopy of of Passing Certificates (With Original)

  • Photocopy of Migration Certificate (With Original)

  • Transfer Certificate (TC) for UP Board student.

  • 4 recent Passport size photographs

  • Gap Year Certificate(In case Gap of 1 or 2 years after XII)

  • Caste certificate (if applicable)

  • Domicile certificate (if belongs to other then U.P.)

  • Character certificate

  • Aadhar card

    WHY

    BSC - MICROBIOLOGY ?


    Microbiology is a branch of science that turns out many imaginations into reality. Microbiology is the study of microorganisms, which are too small to be seen with the naked eye, including bacteria, algae, protozoa, and fungi, as well as acellular agents, such as viruses and prions.


    Microbiology is making significant contributions to the global society. By studying the components of living cells, we can learn how to control, fix and modify them to our benefit. This course aims to provide you the knowledge and practical skills to enable the learner to enter the exciting world of Microbiology. With the conceptual knowledge, living system can be manipulated to create efficient, cost-effective processes for the industrial generation of innovative products.

    WHY

    BSC -BIOTECHNOLOGY ?


    Biotechnology deals with techniques of using live micro-organisms, plant, animal cells or the components from organisms to produce products and processes useful to human beings. It is an interdisciplinary science which imparts many opportunities to meet the challenges of the global scenario. Today it becomes the major scientific thrust both academically and commercially.


    Biotechnology is making significant contributions to the global society. By studying the components of living cells, we can learn how to control, fix and modify them to our benefit.
